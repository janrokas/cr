Clash Royale - one of the best games for mobile phones ever!



Clash Roayle( mobile, multiplayer, real-time strategy video game) was developed and published by Supercell. It was globaly released on March 2, 2016 for iOS and Android. It grew very fast and became extremely famous. The game reached $1 billion in revenue in less than a year on the market!
CR is a tower rush video game which puts players in games featuring two or four players (1v1 or 2v2). The objective is to destroy the biggest amount of opposing towers, with the destruction of the "King's Tower" being an instantaneous win. After 3 minutes, if both of the players or both of the teams have an equal amount of crowns or none at all, the match continues automatically onto overtime and the player who destroys the enemy tower first wins. After overtime, a draw occurs.
